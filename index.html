Bio

I'm Nikos, a Security Engineer and post-student of the department of informatics at Athens Univercity of Economics and Business. After graduating from school, 2nd lyceum in Alimos(southern department of Athens), it was time for me to decide what steps I'm going to take considering my career future.
Curious as to how this little machines can produce so much work, enough to make everyone depending on them, I decided to follow the path of computer science and since then I haven't looked back. It's been an amazing experience for me and each day I learn something new. My hunger for exploring every inch of this science may be the reason why I'm still not aware of what exactly should I focus on. Theses days, taking into consideration my likes and goals, I focus on security and cloud engineering which seem to suit me better, but who knows what the future holds for me. I guess we'll learn in the process! Take a better look of me by searching the rest of the stuff and feel free to contact me for more information.
